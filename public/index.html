Three Key Factors influencing the mental health of International Students

Features

We analyzed many students from various countries to give you three of the factors that affect mental health of International student

First

A reluctant to seek help or unable to access resources to find suitable assistance. Dealing with cultural stigmas, trust and losing face through seeking traditional support services.

Second

Living off-campus and unfamiliar independence including cooking, cleaning, budgeting, transport, relationships and employment.

Third

Unfamiliar academic and social environment. Language and cultural challenges. Lack of problem solving skills and distinct lack of social network./section>
